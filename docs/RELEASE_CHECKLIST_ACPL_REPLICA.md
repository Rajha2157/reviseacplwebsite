# Release Checklist - ACPL Replica

## Content and Structure
- Confirm all planned pages exist in frontend directory.
- Confirm course names and price labels are present.
- Confirm careers and jobs pathways are present.

## Design and Branding
- Confirm approved rebrand palette applied globally.
- Confirm visual style is clearly distinct from source website.
- Confirm responsive behavior on mobile/tablet/desktop.

## QA
- Execute QA test matrix.
- Resolve critical/high defects.
- Re-run navigation and policy page checks.

## Legal and Compliance
- Replace policy placeholders with legal-approved final text.
- Confirm Terms/Refund/Delivery wording approved by stakeholder.

## Deployment Readiness
- Confirm .gitignore excludes temporary/build artifacts.
- Remove temporary local files not needed for release.
- Publish static frontend package to hosting target.

## Hypercare
- Monitor first 48 hours for broken links and display issues.
- Capture user feedback and log phase-2 enhancements.
